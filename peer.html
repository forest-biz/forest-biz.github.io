Peer Learning

Connecting forest businesses with an emphasis on local expertise

Peer Learning Network

The Forest Business Alliance facilitates peer-to-peer learning opportunities that connect forest businesses, entrepreneurs, and organizations across California. Our approach emphasizes local expertise and practical knowledge sharing to build a stronger forest products industry.

What We Offer

  • Networking events connecting forest business professionals
  • Peer mentorship programs pairing experienced operators with new ventures
  • Field tours and site visits to successful operations
  • Discussion groups focused on specific challenges and opportunities
  • Knowledge sharing platforms for best practices and lessons learned

Get Involved

Whether you're an established business owner or just starting out, there are opportunities to both learn from and contribute to our peer learning community. Contact us to learn more about upcoming events and how to participate.
