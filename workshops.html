Approach

FBA creates workshops based on capacity and skill needs for businesses and organizations working in the forest health and forest products sectors. Workshops are typically led be experts, but our philosophy of peer-learning and participatory approaches maximizes learning and networking.

Startup Pathways

The business world is full of startup stories and legends but theyâ€™re not focused on businesses in the wood products industry. Starting a wood products business in California is exceptionally challenging given the lack of infrastructure, permitting, and difficulty in obtaining long-term (>3 years) feedstock contracts from government agencies such as the Forest Service. Funding and finance is challenging given lack of lender expertise in the field and investors wanting returns on a shorter time period. We offer lessons and approaches for new businesses negotiating this challenging environment along with funding and non-funding resources that can pave the way to success.
